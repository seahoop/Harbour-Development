About Harbour Development

Harbour Development is a Seattle real estate investment company focused on value-add residential, multifamily, DADU, and small-bay industrial assets. We acquire properties, improve them through disciplined construction, and hold them as long-term rentals that build enduring value.

What We Do

  • Acquisitions — Identifying undervalued properties with long-term upside
  • Renovation & Development — Efficient construction across residential and heavy industrial
  • Long-Term Ownership — Holding assets for stable cashflow and appreciation
  • Industrial Expansion — Growing into industrial development
